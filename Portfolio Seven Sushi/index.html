About

Located only steps away from our Bar & Grill in the heart of downtown Belleville; Seven Sushi offers an extensive menu of gourmet sushi, unique appetizers, and an array of delicious cocktails to please your taste buds. Not just a destination for lunch and dinner, Seven Sushi provides an atmosphere and decor perfect for late-night fun. Come in for great drinks, fresh music, dancing, and a night life experience like no other on Main Street.

Unfamiliar Terms

  • Eel Sauce

    Sugar, Dashi, Soy Sauce, Rice Wine, Cornstarch, Mirin

  • Oshinko

    Japanese Pickled Radish

  • Wakame

    Edible Seaweed

  • Dashi

    Fish Soup Stock

  • Kampyo

    Dried shavings of Calabash, a type of gourd

  • Bonito Flakes

    ried, Fermented, Smoked Skipjack Tuna

  • Panzu Sauce

    Citrus-based sauce

  • Mochi

    Rice Cake (soft)

  • Mirin

    Rice Wine (lower alcohol than sake and higher sugar content)
